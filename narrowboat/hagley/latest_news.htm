Hagley - Latest News

Launch Video

Hagley was launched at midday on 3rd March 2002 after 2.25years of rebuilding.

Ken Keay Award

Much to the amazement of all concerned, Hagley won the Ken Keay award for 2002. This is awarded by the Historic Narrow Boat Owners Club annually to the wooden boat they consider has been most improved in the preceding year. The award was presented at the Birmingham inland waterways festival and even made it into Canal Boat and Waterways World.
