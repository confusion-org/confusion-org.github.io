Shropshire Union Nantwich 92 14 19 6.5 Large town, based on the salt industry.
S.U. Audlem Wharf 67 19.3 29 9.5 Moored right outside the Shroppie Fly. This pub has some rather twee features, like the old railway crane outside on the wharf, painted up to look as though it belongs on a canal and the bar shaped like a narrowboat. However the food is really good and imaginative and the pub runs a book exchange scheme resulting in one of the strangest selections of books to be found anywhere. Also sells Flowers' Original and doesn't object to appallingly bad pool players!

B.C.N. Main Line Horseley Fields Junction 69 11 10 4.5 The W&E winds off to the north here. A classic contour canal passing many collieries it used to join up with the Coventry canal. The top six miles are now shut and the canal is little used, but restoration work is underway on this through route. The canal will close again briefly when the wall which I built collapses and has to be taken out!
